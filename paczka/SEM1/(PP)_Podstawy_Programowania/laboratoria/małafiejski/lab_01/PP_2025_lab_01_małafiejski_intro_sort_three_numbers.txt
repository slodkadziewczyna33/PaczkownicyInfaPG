OOP3SORT - Sort three numbers

Write the program that sorts three numbers.
Input

There are given three integer numbers, each number in the separate line. One may assume that all the numbers are between -1000000 and 1000000.
Output

Write three numbers from the input in the non-decreasing order. Each number must be in the separate line.
Example

Input:
    105
    -68
    12

Output:
    -68
    12
    105
