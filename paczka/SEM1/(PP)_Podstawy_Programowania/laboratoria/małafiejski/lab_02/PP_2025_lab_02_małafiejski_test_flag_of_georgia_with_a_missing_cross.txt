PCPGFLAG2 - Flag of Georgia with a missing cross

Write a flag of Georgia with stars to the output. But be careful, there is missing one small cross! In the input there are two numbers k, 1<=k<=20 and s, 1<=s<=4. The flag is within a square of size n x n, where n=4*k+14, but with a missing a small cross in the field denoted by s, where the fields are numbered as follows:

*********
* 1 * 2 *
*********
* 3 * 4 *
*********

In the following there is an example for k equal to 1 and 2, and different s.
Example

Input:
1 1

Output:
******************
*       **       *
*       **       *
*       **   *   *
*       **  ***  *
*       **   *   *
*       **       *
*       **       *
******************
******************
*       **       *
*       **       *
*   *   **   *   *
*  ***  **  ***  *
*   *   **   *   *
*       **       *
*       **       *
******************

Input:
2 4

Output:
**********************
*         **         *
*         **         *
*         **         *
*    *    **    *    *
*   ***   **   ***   *
*    *    **    *    *
*         **         *
*         **         *
*         **         *
**********************
**********************
*         **         *
*         **         *
*         **         *
*    *    **         *
*   ***   **         *
*    *    **         *
*         **         *
*         **         *
*         **         *
**********************
