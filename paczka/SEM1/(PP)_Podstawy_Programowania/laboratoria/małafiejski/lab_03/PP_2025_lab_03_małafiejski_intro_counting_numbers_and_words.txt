OOPCNW - Counting numbers and words

Write the program that counts how many words and numbers are in a given line.
Input

There are given at most 1000 data sets. Each data set consists from the numbers and words (letters 'a'-'z' and 'A'-'Z') separated by spaces.
Output

The number of numbers and words in each line.
Example

Input:
Dato has 2 cats and 3 dogs
2 plus 2 is equal to 4

 

Output:
2 5
3 4
