SPSCOUNT - Simple Counter

Write the program that counts the number of lines, words and characters of a given text.
Input

Text provided in the input is a sequence of lines. Each line consists of a sequence of whitespaces, words and dots, with a newline at the end. Word is a sequence of letters (a-zA-Z).
Output

The number of lines, words and characters.
Examples

Input:
It is the first line.
Second line... oh..no..no no
Output:
2 11 51

Input:
The boy stood on the burning deck
shelling peanuts by the peck
Output:
2 12 63
